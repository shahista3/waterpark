Welcome to Central India’s first Oceanic theme Water Park and Resort!


A land full of endless magic, breath taking views, family friendly thrills, thunder parks and adventurous activities. At lighthouse , we ensure that every guest experiences the most memorable time with tons of activities along with 40 + attractions making it the most ideal destination for sharing small moments and for celebrating big wonders. Magnificently built, the park contains 4 Big Pools, 15+ Adventurous water slides , Amusement park , Agro park ,Cultural and Spiritual park , elegantly designed cottages, and a dedicated food zone catering to varied taste buds.

WATER PARK

Thrill, chill and spill on high energy slides, and take over a whole new twisting, turning, and sliding experience as you dance to the rhythm of high fidelity sounds and psychedelic laser lights.
